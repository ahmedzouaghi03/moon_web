ABOUT US

Welcome to Moon Innovation, where dreams meet technology, and innovative solutions come alive. We are a trailblazing tech company based in Sfax, Tunisia, that was founded in 2023. A unique collaboration between two forward-thinking engineers and a visionary teacher laid the foundation for our company, creating a perfect synergy of technical prowess and educational insight.

 Our team at Moon Innovation has a deep-rooted belief in the power of automation and its potential to revolutionize our lives, from Earth to air, and extending even into the depths of water. We specialize in creating autonomous robots that can function and adapt in varied environments,
each engineered to simplify tasks and improve efficiencies.

 Our products range from terrestrial robots designed to navigate challenging terrains on Earth, to advanced marine robots capable of deep-sea exploration,
to aerial drones equipped to traverse the skies. Every machine we build is a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and relentless pursuit of technological excellence.

 Our founders' unique background, combining the meticulous mindset of engineers with the insightful perspective of a teacher, enables us to focus not just on creating cutting-edge technology, but also ensuring that our innovations are meaningful, accessible, and beneficial to society.

 At Moon Innovation, we believe in the transformative power of technology. We are committed to pushing boundaries, challenging norms, and harnessing the potential of automation to change the world. Through our creations, we strive to make a significant impact, not only in Tunisia but on a global scale.

 Join us as we embark on a journey of innovation and exploration, pushing the frontiers of technology and shaping a future where robots and automation are an integral part of our lives. Together, we will write a new chapter in the story of progress, driven by the power of Moon Innovation.
